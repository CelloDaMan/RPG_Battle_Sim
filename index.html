Lore: You are Garfeldi the cat and you must fight your way through enemies to restore faith in humanity.

Learn more about Garfeldi here

Gameplay: Fight Enemies and Level up! There are 10 enemies to fight in a row, defeat them all to win.

Upon leveling up, your stats will randomly increase with HP increasing by 1-3, Attack by 0-2, and Defense by 0-1. This means there's a possibility of not beating able to defeat all the enemies, so Keep Gambling!

At Level 3, you will unlock Full Force, a skill that does 1.5x your normal damage and does half the damage you deal back to you as recoil.

At Level 10, you will unlock Life Leech, a skill that does half your normal damage, but heals you for half the damage you deal.
